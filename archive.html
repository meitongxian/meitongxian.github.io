Maggie Meitong Xian is a designer interested in systems of all sorts, with experiences spanning identity, editorial, and web. Currently, she’s looking forward to finishing her BFA in Graphic Design at Rhode Island School of Design in Spring 2026. You’ll most likely find her near a body of water, but home will always be under the long white clouds of Aotearoa New Zealand.

Resume and PDF portfolio available upon request, reach out at maggiemxian@gmail.com.
