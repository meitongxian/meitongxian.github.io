ON BEING AND BUILDING

Publication, Code
Spring 2025–

Interview conducted and transcribed with Lea Seo.

An interview with designer/developer Tiger Dingsun on being a multi-disciplinary creative and building personal sensibilities. Initially designed as a 4-page newspaper, then expanded as a micro-site in full RGB hyperlinked iframe glory.

Drawing from the content of the interview and Tiger’s work, visual elements are inspired by Are.na blocks, Minecraft skies, and windows in all forms. Themes such as accumulation and legibility are represented by a generative cloud animation, blur interactions, and a custom scroll bar mapped to the length of the interview.

Fully hand-coded in HTML/CSS/JS.
