Maggie Meitong Xian is a designer interested in systems of all sorts, with experiences spanning identity, editorial, and web. She will receive her BFA in Graphic Design from Rhode Island School of Design in Spring 2026, with minors in Computation and Sustainability. You’ll most likely find her near a body of water, but home will always be under the long white clouds of Aotearoa New Zealand.

Resume and PDF portfolio available upon request, reach out at maggiemxian@gmail.com.
