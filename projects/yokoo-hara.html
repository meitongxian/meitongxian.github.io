Maggie Meitong Xian 🔖

is pursuing a BFA in Graphic Design, Computation, & Sustainability @ Rhode Island School of Design.

Striving to design with care and empathy, her work is often informed by research and the interplay of physical and digital. Raised in Aotearoa New Zealand, you’ll most likely find her near a body of water.

Currently @ RISD Design Guild, & Design Lead @ Better World by Design.
