is pursuing a BFA in Graphic Design @ Rhode Island School of Design.

DEBATES IN AI

Identity
Spring 2024

With Hannah Jeong & Li Huang @ RISD Design Guild.

Rhode Island School of Design’s Debates in AI was a symposium held April 11–12, 2024, that invited artists worldwide to address the multifaceted dimensions of artificial intelligence (AI) and its profound impact on creative disciplines. The identity was based upon the visual language of information processing and the fuzzy ambiguities of AI generated letterforms.
